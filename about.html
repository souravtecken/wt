About Us


We here at Saviour Machine aim to provide a one of a kind system that eases the life
of a high school student, by providing everything he or she would ever need
to survive high school.

This idea is inspired by our own high school experiences, where we
faced alot of difficulties in scheduling and tasking our already jam-packed
lives.

The main aim of this platform is to consolidate resources such as
reminders, calenders, timetables etc. while also providing a way to quickly glance
at elements and thier characteristics and a calculator.
